The LLAMA tests (Clone)

A suite of language learning tests inspired by the original LLAMA battery.

This open‑source clone provides a series of four language learning tasks. Each task assesses a different aspect of your ability to learn and remember linguistic information. While the content here is inspired by the original LLAMA tests, the implementation is independent and self‑contained.

We recommend completing the tasks in the order shown below. Each link will take you to a separate page with its own instructions and testing interface.

  • LLAMA D – Lexical Recognition
  • LLAMA B – Vocabulary Learning
  • LLAMA E – Sound–Symbol Mapping
  • LLAMA F – Grammar Induction

Version 0.1 • This clone is provided under an open‑source license. Feel free to modify and extend it to suit your research or teaching requirements.
